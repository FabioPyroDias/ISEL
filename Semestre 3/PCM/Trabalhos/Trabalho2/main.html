About

My name is Fábio, but I am also known as Pyro amongst friends and games. At the age of six, I realized what I wanted to do for the rest of my life whilst playing Spyro: Enter the Dragonfly. Engulged in this fantastic virtual world, I knew I wanted to be a game developer and, ever since then, that's what I've been aiming for.

Presently I am a programmer, game designer and an avid player in my spare time. I favour a collaborative work environment and I am always eager to learn!
